मेरी सॉरी 💌 A Letter from the Heart

मेरी प्यारी Mishri ❤️,

Please mujhe ek baar maaf kar do. Mujhse jo bhi hua, uski poori zimmedari meri hai. Main koi bahana nahi banaunga, kyunki galti meri hi thi. Mujhe sach mein bahut bura lag raha hai ki meri wajah se tumhe dukh hua. Main jab se yeh sab hua hai tab se bas isi baat ka afsos hai ki maine bina soche-samjhe aisi baatein keh di jo kabhi nahi kehni chahiye thi.

Main dil se tumse bahut pyaar karta hoon. Kabhi-kabhi gusse ya emotions mein main bina soche bol deta hoon, aur baad mein realize hota hai ki meri baaton ne tumhe kitni takleef di hogi. Yeh meri kami hai, aur main isse badalna chahta hoon. Main wada karta hoon ki apni in aadaton par kaam karunga aur khud ko pehle se behtar insaan banaunga.

Tum mere liye sirf ek insaan nahi ho. Tum meri zindagi ka bahut hi special hissa ho. Tumhari smile, tumhari care, tumhara nature... sab kuch mere liye bahut precious hai. Aur haan, tum bahut hi beautiful ho — sirf dekhne mein hi nahi, balki dil se bhi. Shayad main har baar apni feelings sahi tarah se express nahi kar pata, lekin meri feelings hamesha sach hoti hain.

💔 ➡️ ❤️‍🩹 ➡️ ❤️

Main nahi chahta ki meri ek galti humare rishte se zyada badi ban jaaye. Agar mumkin ho, to is baar bhi mujhe maaf kar do. Main tumhara trust dobara jeetna chahta hoon. Main actions se dikhana chahta hoon ki main waqai badal sakta hoon.

Please ek mauka aur de do. Main tumhe kabhi jaan-boojhkar hurt nahi karna chahta tha aur na hi kabhi chahunga. Mujhe bas tumhari muskurahat wapas dekhni hai.

I am really, really sorry, Mishri 💔

Please forgive me. I love you with all my heart,
and I never want to lose someone as special as you. 💖

Hamesha tumhara, — Sumit ❤️
